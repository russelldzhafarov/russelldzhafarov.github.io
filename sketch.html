Sympli Plugin for Sketch

Sympli plugin for Sketch offers a quick and effective way to hand over your designs to your developers. Sympli Handoff turns creating the visual specs and assets for your design handoff into a one-click action.

With the Sympli plugin for Sketch you have a centralized, unbreakable, platform-agnostic source of truth that enables your development team to access all your designs projects and tokens and download pre-generated code snippets.
